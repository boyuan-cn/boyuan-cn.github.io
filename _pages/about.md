I am a Group Lead in the AI for Health (AI4Health) direction at Lingang Lab. This initiative focuses on applying deep learning with national real-world medical data to solve practical problems in drug discovery and medical informatics.

<span style="color:SkyBlue">袁博，2026年全职加入临港国家实验室做医疗信息大组的方向负责人。主要的方向是将AI应用在大规模临床数据上，在国家队层面建立大模型助力药物开发和国家级AI辅助原子化健康管理平台。团队快速扩张中，所以[招人](/recruiting/)，从RA、研究生、博后到独立PI都招。

Our current work involves building and scaling AI models that integrate genomic data with clinical insights to accelerate therapeutic development. 

<span style="color:SkyBlue">我们关于药物研发和医疗大数据相关的AI什么都做。</span>

My previous projects span broadly from academic research to industrial translation, including roles at Harvard/MIT/Broad/DFCI, as well as at DeepMind (Science team, or the AlphaFold team), Google (brain genomics), Tencent (AI Lab), and Merck (PK/PD and data sciences team).

<span style="color:SkyBlue">以前也在学术圈和工业界之间反复横跳，所以知道怎么混paper，也知道怎么混公司。混过DeepMind Sciences那种天马行空组，也在Merck默沙东这种真实世界的场景进行过实际的转化落地。</span>

These diverse experiences inform my approach to bridging computational methods with real-world biological applications.

<span style="color:SkyBlue">又吹了一遍。</span>

I hold a Ph.D. from Harvard University, co-advised by Aviv Regev, Chris Sander and Debora Marks.

<span style="color:SkyBlue">我有个哈佛的博士学位，拿发际线换的，主要在填表的时候起到一个装饰的作用。因为多方面原因（感兴趣的话可以DM我了解），博士期间前后有过三个导师，是为三姓PhD。</span>

I look forward to exchanging ideas and forging collaborations with both industry pioneers and the diverse talents driving our field forward.

<span style="color:SkyBlue">各位大佬带带，谢谢。</span>
